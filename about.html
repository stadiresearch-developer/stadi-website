Who We Are

Stadi means skill or expertise in Kiswahili. That is exactly what we bring.

Stadi Research & Analytics began as a specialist research and survey firm, built on the conviction that great decisions require great evidence. Over the years, we have partnered with development organisations, government agencies, agribusinesses, and private companies across East Africa and the continent.

As the data landscape changed — and as the opportunities created by AI and machine learning became tangible — we expanded our scope. We are now not only research partners; we are builders. We develop data solutions that collect, trace, analyse, and predict.
