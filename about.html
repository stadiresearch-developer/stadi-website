Our Story

From specialist research firm to full-spectrum data partner.

Stadi Research & Analytics began as a specialist research and survey firm, built on the conviction that great decisions require great evidence. Over the years, we have partnered with development organisations, government agencies, agribusinesses, and private companies across East Africa and the continent, delivering surveys, evaluations, and analytics that inform strategy, secure funding, and improve outcomes.

As the data landscape changed — and as the opportunities created by AI and machine learning became tangible — we expanded our scope. We are now not only research partners; we are builders. We develop data solutions that collect, trace, analyse, and predict.

Our name, Stadi, means skill or expertise in Kiswahili. That is exactly what we bring: the skill to ask the right questions, and the expertise to build the right solutions.
