Service

Solutions & Application Development

We design and build custom software solutions — from interactive dashboards and data platforms to tailored web and mobile applications. Our engineering team turns research findings and analytics into reliable, user-friendly products that organisations use every day.

Whether you need a data collection app, a real-time monitoring dashboard, or a full-stack platform, we pair domain expertise with modern tooling to ship products that put insights directly into the hands of the people who need them.

Service

Baseline Survey

We have the best expertise in designing and implementing baseline surveys, mid-term, outcome and impact assessments of policy, projects and programmes. We also support development of M&E frameworks, indicators and M&E work plans.

Our rigorous methodology ensures that every data point collected is accurate, actionable, and aligned with your programme objectives and donor requirements.

Service

Business Analytics

At Stadi Research and Analytics, our business analytics services unlock valuable insights for data-driven strategies and informed decision-making. With advanced tools and methodologies, we help businesses uncover trends, optimize operations, and identify growth opportunities.

From market research to customer segmentation and competitive analysis, we deliver intelligence that drives measurable business outcomes.

Service

Data Analytics & Visualization

We employ statistical and machine learning techniques to analyze, visualize and report data insights. We also develop robust models to help in identification of patterns in the data and perform predictive analytics.

Our approach ensures that data tells a clear story — through interactive dashboards, automated reports, and compelling visualisations that enable stakeholders to make confident, informed decisions.

Service

Training & Capacity Development

We offer comprehensive training and capacity development programs, equipping clients with the essential skills and knowledge to excel in research, data collection, M&E, and predictive analytics.

Our expert-led training empowers individuals and organizations to leverage data effectively, drive innovation, and achieve their goals with confidence.

Service

Questionnaire Design & Data Collection

We design and script questionnaires using robust mobile toolkits including ODK/KoBo Toolbox, Survey CTO and CSPro, and integrate both online and mobile tools to collect reliable, valid data.

Our end-to-end data collection solutions cover instrument design, enumerator training, field supervision, data quality assurance, and real-time monitoring — ensuring the foundation for sound decision-making.
